Obstacle & Solution Center

💰 Financial Constraints

Common Issues:

  • Limited money for courses and resources
  • Need to work long hours for survival
  • Can't afford gym membership or equipment
  • No budget for healthy food and supplements

Solutions:

  • Use free resources: YouTube, Coursera audit, Khan Academy
  • Apply for scholarships and free programs
  • Bodyweight exercises at home, outdoor running
  • Budget meal planning with affordable nutritious options
  • Seek part-time remote work as skills improve

⏰ Time Management

Common Issues:

  • Long work hours for delivery job
  • Physical and mental fatigue after work
  • Multiple goals competing for time
  • Difficulty maintaining consistent schedule

Solutions:

  • Wake up 1 hour earlier for focused study time
  • Use delivery breaks for audio learning
  • Time-block specific activities with strict boundaries
  • Prioritize high-impact activities first
  • Use micro-learning sessions (15-30 mins)

🎓 Lack of Experience

Common Issues:

  • No prior knowledge in data science
  • Never prepared for competitive exams
  • No fitness or MMA background
  • Overwhelmed by amount to learn

Solutions:

  • Start with absolute beginner courses
  • Find online study groups and mentors
  • Follow structured beginner-friendly programs
  • Document learning journey for motivation
  • Celebrate small wins and progress milestones
